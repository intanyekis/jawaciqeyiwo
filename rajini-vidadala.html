Rajini Vidadala (24 June 1988) is an Indian government servant, politician, and MLA from Chilakaluripet Guntur, Andhra Pradesh. In present, she serves the position of Member of the Legislative Assembly (MLA) for the Chilakaluripeta Assembly constituency.

Apart from this, Vidadala is also a politician. She is a member of the Yuvajana Sramika Rythu Congress Party (YSRCP). Rajini becomes the MLA of the Chilakaluripet Constituency in the year 2019. She won the 2019 election with the majority of 8000 + votes in her favor.

Rajini earned the seat of MLAby defeating her opponent “Prathipati Pulla Rao” in the elections. She also acts as a philanthropist. Vidadala always ready to help the needy peoples.
