Travis Kelces Sister-In-Law Reveals Why She Feels Guilty That Hes Dating Taylor Swift

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  618 words  |  Martina Birk

Highlights

  • Kylie Kelce finds it odd that people are so interested in her family's personal life.
  • She feels guilty that people are invested in what she's doing, as she finds it uninteresting.
  • Kylie appreciates the support but thinks that her everyday life is not that fascinating.

The Kelce family has been thrust into the spotlight since Travis Kelce and Taylor Swift debuted their romance in September. Now, Travis’ sister-in-law Kylie Kelce is opening up about what it’s been like to be all over the headlines.

During an appearance on Good Morning America, the 31-year-old admitted she finds it odd that people are so invested in her family’s personal life, especially since she feels there’s not much of interest.

Kylie Kelce Doesn’t Understand Why People Are Interested In Her Life

“I feel almost a little guilty that people are so interested and invested in what I’m doing,” Kylie, explained. “I’m like, ‘I am trying to scrape applesauce off the couch. This is not that fascinating.’ So, it’s funny to me. I deeply appreciate the support.”

Kylie is married to Travis’ older brother Jason Kelce. Together, they have three daughters: Wyatt, 4, Elliotte, 2, and Bennett, 11 months. While the Kelce brothers are both NFL stars, Kylie has a relatively normal job in comparison – she’s a high school field hockey coach in Philadelphia.

Kylie is often in the stands at Chiefs games alongside Jason and her mother-in-law Donna Kelce. Now, the Kelce family has been increasingly joined by Taylor, who has made a point of attending as many of Travis’ games as she can make work.

Recently, fans praised Kylie for how she handled her husband Jason’s shirtless moment when the Chiefs played the Buffalo Bills. When the Chiefs won a touchdown, Jason was clearly elated, jumping out of their suite with a beer in hand, only to land directly in the stands at the New York venue (which was primarily filled with Bills fans).

Taylor Reportedly Gets Along Well With Travis’ Family

Speaking to Good Morning America, Kylie explained her calm approach to her husband’s headline-making reaction, admitting he told her beforehand it was his plan.

“He said to me, ‘I’m going to, I’m going to take my shirt off and I’m gonna jump out,'” Kylie recalled. “And I was like, ‘I don’t think that’s the best idea.’ But I think that it was that continued energy from when he was hyping himself up.”

“So, the first time he jumped out of the suite, I was, you know what, go ahead. That’s my husband! And then the second and third time he did it, I was like, I’m gonna need you to get back in because the poor cops that are out front trying to keep the chaos at a minimum, are, you’re making their jobs harder,” the mother-of-three said. “So, at first, I was all for it. And then I had to try and reel him back in.”

With reports that Taylor is getting along swimmingly with the Kelces, it appears she’s found a friend in Kylie, even if Kylie isn’t used to the attention that comes with hanging out with a global pop star.

Travis and Taylor continued to be bombarded with engagement and even pregnancy rumors, so Taylor may become an official member of the Kelce family before fans know it.
