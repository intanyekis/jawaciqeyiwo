Laila Rouass Biography.

Laila Abdesselam Rouass is a British actress best known for her roles as Amber Gates in Footballers’ Wives and Sahira Shah in Holby City. She has also starred in Primeval and Spooks and been a participant on Strictly Come Dancing, whereby she finished fourth.

Laila Rouass Age.

Laila Abdesselam Rouass was born on June 22nd, 1971 in Stepney London. She is 48 years old.  Her birth sign is Cancer.

Laila Rouass Husband

Rouass married Abdeslam Rouass in 1990 in Tower Hamlets, London and divorced in 2003. She went through a religious ceremony with Nasir Khan in 2005 but were never legally married. They broke up shortly after she gave birth to her daughter. Laila then dated Scottish chef James Petrie for about one year and went their separate ways. After they broke up in 2011, Rouass took a break from dating in order to concentrate on her young daughter and her career. She then met snooker player Ronnie O’Sullivan in 2012 unexpectedly while house hunting. They instantly fell in love and got engaged in February 2013.

Laila Rouass India

Rouass’ first film was City of Dreams produced by Feroze Nadiadwala, in which she starred alongside Lisa Ray and Saeed Jaffery. During the years that she was based in India, early in her career, she acted in some Indian films, Dev Benegal’s Split Wide Open (1999) and Aditya Bhattacharya’s Indo-Italian Senso Unico (1999).

Rouass starred in Jag Mhundra’s controversial film, Bawandar (English title; The Sand Storm), about revenge rapes in Rajasthan in 2009, and she made her English-language film debut in 2002 with a small role in The Four Feathers opposite Heath Ledger. Rouass also appeared in the independent British film Shoot on Sight (2007) opposite Brian Cox, Om Puri, and Sadie Frost. She appeared in two films in 2008, Freebird and Apron Strings.

Strictly Come Dancing.

Laila participated in the BBC One reality show on the seventh series of Strictly Come Dancing. She was paired with professional dancer Anton Du Beke and alongside Footballers’ Wives co-star Zöe Lucker. They made it to the last four before being voted off the show on 6 December 2009.
