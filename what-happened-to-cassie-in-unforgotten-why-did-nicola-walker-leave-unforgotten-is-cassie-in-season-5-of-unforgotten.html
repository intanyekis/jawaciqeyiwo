Unforgotten

Unforgotten is a British crime drama television series that follows a team of London detectives as they solve cold cases of disappearance and murder. Each series consists of six episodes, with Series 1 to 4 airing in the UK in 2015, 2017, 2018, and 2021. A fifth series was announced on 30 March 2021, with Sinéad Keenan replacing Nicola Walker as DCI Jess James alongside Sanjeev Bhaskar. Each series deals with a new case, introducing seemingly unconnected characters who are gradually revealed to have some relationship with the victim. The emotional ramifications of the crime on the lives of those affected are also explored. Unforgotten has received critical acclaim, with Tom Courtenay and Mark Bonnar winning BAFTA TV Awards for their roles in the first and second series, respectively.
