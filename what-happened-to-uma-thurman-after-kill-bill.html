What Happened To Uma Thurman After 'Kill Bill'?

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  938 words  |  Martina Birk

Actress Uma Thurman rose to international fame with her role as Mia Wallace in Quentin Tarantino's 1994 movie Pulp Fiction. Since then, Thurman starred in numerous blockbusters but many fans regard her portrayal of The Bride in Quentin Tarantino's Kill Bill: Volume 1 and 2 from 2003 and 2004 as her most important work of art.

Today, we're taking a look at everything the actress has been up to. From which movies and shows Uma Thurman starred in to what her current upcoming projects are — keep on scrolling to find out!

10 In 2005 She Starred In The Crime Comedy 'Be Cool'

We're kicking the list off in 2005 when Uma Thurman starred in the crime-comedy Be Cool. In it, she portrayed Edie Athens and she starred alongside John Travolta, Vince Vaughn, Cedric the Entertainer, Andre Benjamin, Steven Tyler, Robert Pastorelli, Christina Milian, Harvey Keitel, The Rock, and Danny DeVito. Currently, Be Cool — which tells the story of mobster Chili Palmer's entrance into the music industry — has a 5.6 rating on IMDb.

9 And A Year Later Fans Could See Her In The Superhero Comedy 'My Super Ex-Girlfriend'

Next on the list is the superhero comedy My Super Ex-Girlfriend in which Uma Thurman portrays Jennifer Johnson / G-Girl. Besides the actress, the movie also stars Luke Wilson, Anna Faris, Eddie Izzard, Rainn Wilson, Wanda Sykes, Stelio Savante, Mike Iorio, Mark Consuelos, and Margaret Anne Florence. My Super Ex-Girlfriend tells the story of a superhero making the life of her ex miserable and it currently has a 5.1 rating on IMDb.

8 In 2010 Thurman Appeared In The Fantasy Movie 'Percy Jackson & The Olympians: The Lightning Thief'

In 2010 fans could see Uma Thurman in the action fantasy movie Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief. In it, Thurman portrayed Medusa and she starred alongside Logan Lerman, Brandon T. Jackson, Alexandra Daddario, Sean Bean, Pierce Brosnan, Steve Coogan, Rosario Dawson, Catherine Keener, Kevin McKidd, and Joe Pantoliano.

Currently, Percy Jackson & the Olympians: The Lightning Thief — which tells the story of a teenager discovering he's the descendant of a Greek god — has a 5.9 rating on IMDb.

7 Two Years Later She Joined The Cast Of The NBC Musical Show 'Smash'

In 2012 Uma Thurman joined the cast of the musical drama show Smash for five episodes. In them, the actress portrayed Rebecca Duvall and she starred alongside Debra Messing, Jack Davenport, Katharine McPhee, Christian Borle, Megan Hilty, Anjelica Huston, Leslie Odom Jr., Jeremy Jordan, Krysta Rodriguez, Andy Mientus, and Brian d'Arcy James. Smash ran for two seasons and it currently has a 7.7 rating on IMDb.

6 In 2013 She Was A Part Of The Cast Of Lars Von Trier's 'Nymphomaniac'

Let's move on to the 2013 two-part erotic art movie Nymphomaniac written and directed by Lars von Trier. In it, Uma Thurman portrayed Mrs. H and she starred alongside Charlotte Gainsbourg, Stacy Martin, Stellan Skarsgård, Shia LaBeouf, Christian Slater, Jamie Bell, Willem Dafoe, Mia Goth, Sophie Kennedy Clark, and Connie Nielsen. Currently, the first part of Nymphomaniac has a 6.9 rating while the second one has a 6.7 rating on IMDb.

5 In 2015 She Starred In The Miniseries 'The Slap'

In 2015 the actress portrayed Anouk Latham in the drama miniseries The Slap. Besides Thurman, the miniseries also starred Brian Cox, Melissa George, Thandiwe Newton, Zachary Quinto, Thomas Sadoski, and Peter Sarsgaard. The Slap tells the story of the aftermath of a birthday party and it currently has a 7.6 rating on IMDb.

4 And In 2017 Fans Could See Her On The Dark Comedy Show 'Imposters'

Another show that Uma Thurman could be seen in is the dark comedy show Imposters which premiered in 2017 and ran for two seasons. In it, the actress played Lenny Cohen and she starred alongside Inbar Lavi, Rob Heaps, Parker Young, Marianne Rendón, Stephen Bishop, Brian Benben, and Katherine LaNasa. Currently, Imposters — which tells the story of a female con artist — has a 7.8 rating on IMDb.

3 The Actress Also Appeared In The 2018 Horror Movie 'The House That Jack Built'

In 2018 fans could see the actress in the horror movie The House That Jack Built. In it, Uma Thurman portrayed Lady 1 and she starred alongside Matt Dillon, Bruno Ganz, Siobhan Fallon Hogan, Sofie Grabol. Riley Keough, and Jeremy Davies. Currently, The House That Jack Built — which tells the story of a highly intelligent serial killer — has a 6.8 rating on IMDb.

2 In 2019 Fans Could See The Actress In The Netflix Thriller Show 'Chambers'

Two years ago Uma Thurman starred in the Netflix supernatural horror show Chambers which ran for only one season. In it, she played Nancy Lefevre and she starred alongside Sivan Alyra Rose, Griffin Powell-Arcand, Tony Goldwyn, Lilliya Reid, Nicholas Galitzine, Kyanna Simone Simpson, Lilli Kay, Sarah Mezzanotte, and Marcus LaVoi.

Chambers tells the story of a woman who survived a heart transplant and it currently has a 6.5 rating on IMDb.

1 Lastly, She Currently Has Three Upcoming Projects

And finally, we're wrapping the list up with the fact that Uma Thurman currently has three upcoming projects. The actress is set to play a lead role in the upcoming thriller show Suspicion, and she also has two upcoming movies — Hollywood Stargirl and Tau Ceti Four.
