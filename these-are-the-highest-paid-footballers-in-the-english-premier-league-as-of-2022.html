English Premier League or the EPL is the top level football league system in England. It is contested by 20 clubs and operates on the system of promotion and relegation with English Football League. The seasons typically run from August until May and each of the team is playing for 38 matches. The Premier League is full of the highest paid players in the world of football and it is also the magnet for megastars of this generation. It has the financial pull that every other league can dream of.

The EPL is the envy in the world from the business perspective with major TV deals up to the international broadcasting rights which continues to flood the game with the riches and money. The game is made exciting by the best players among the world which is the reason why they are highly paid. Here’s the highest paid players in the EPL today.
