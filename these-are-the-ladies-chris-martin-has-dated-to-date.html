All The Ladies Coldplay Frontman Chris Martin Has Dated In His Life

Singer-songwriter Chris Martin has been widely known as the loving husband of American actress Gwyneth Paltrow until he was not. The frontman of Coldplay has had a colorful love life before and after his marriage to the Iron Man actress. For the entirety of the Let Somebody Go singer’s career, he has been linked and rumored with numerous actresses and singers, some were confirmed and others just remained rumors. Take a look at the list of celebrities who reportedly dated Chris Martin.

8 Dakota Johnson

Fifty Shades of Grey star Dakota Johnson reportedly started dating Chris Martin in October 2017. The couple seems to have a great relationship going and there were even pregnancy rumors after blue and pink balloons were seen over at the house of Johnson. There were breakup rumors around June 2019 but was quickly dispelled as the pair appeared walking side by side soon after. According to multiple sources, the couple is having major disagreements over the possibility of starting a family. It was believed that singer wanted to start a new family while the actress still wants to explore her career. Although there were numerous breakup rumors, it is unclear whether the two are still together to this day. Martin was recently seen with the actress earlier this year as the singer was seen with Dakota while she was doing a virtual discussion for the film Cha Cha Real Smooth.

7 Annabelle Wallis

English actress Annabelle Wallis started dating Chris Martin in August 2015 however the couple split after two years together. The actress who also dated actor Chris Pine for four years has somewhat an on and off relationship with the frontman of Coldplay. After the two split in 2017, there were reports claiming that Chris Martin rekindled his romantic relationship with the English actress however there was no confirmation on this rumor and Martin was reportedly still with Dakota at this time.

6 Jennifer Lawrence

Just five months after Gwyneth Paltrow filed for divorce, Chris Martin was already seen out and about with The Hunger Games actress Jennifer Lawrence in August 2014. The two reportedly dated from June 2014 until June 2015. The two have split just after four months of dating in October 2014 however rekindled their relationship in 2015. The couple eventually broke up and now Lawrence is happily married and has already welcomed her first child last month while Chris Martin is still looking for someone to settle down with.

5 Alexa Chung

The Hymn For The Weekend singer has reportedly got together with the British writer, blogger and TV personality Alexa Chung in May 2014. Rumors of their supposed relationship resurfaced shortly after the singer has split with his ex-wife Gwyneth Paltrow. The rumor came about after Martin and Chung were spotted seemingly having a good time at a party. Although there were photos of their supposed hookup when they were spotted in a bar, the reps of Martin has denied the allegations and claimed that the two were just friends.

4 Natalie Imbruglia

The Australian-British singer who is best known for her hit songs Torn and Shiver has also dated the lead vocalist of Coldplay. The two singers have dated in 2000 before Martin got married to his wife of ten years, Gwyneth Paltrow. Martin seems to have loved Imbruglia too much that he even wrote songs for her to use for her album. Of course, Martin’s contribution was acknowledged as the singer’s name appeared on the credits of singer's album. However, Chris Martin admitted that he later on regretted giving away the songs to Natalie as the song could have been the best Coldplay song ever, but he gave it away.

3 Dua Lipa

The two brilliant singers have collaborated for Dua Lipa’s song Homesick released in 2017. Homesick is a song which was included in the singer’s eponymous debut studio album in 2017. The song was co-written by the Coldplay frontman Chris Martin and Lipa. Rumors of them hooking up circulated around 2019 and reports say that the two were seen kissing. The Break My Heart singer jokingly said that she lost her sanity at the Glastonbury 2019 after she was spotted cozying with the Coldplay lead vocalist.

2 Kylie Minogue

The two prominent stars reportedly dated back in 2015. According to numerous sources, the two musicians went out for a stroll in the busy streets of London on a Sunday night after Minogue’s Hyde Park performance. The two were even seen linking arms and seems to be very comfortable with each other. Although there were rumors about their supposed relationship, there was never any confirmation regarding their relationship. Kylie Minogue and Chris Martin have reunited recently at the Coldplay's concert held in New Jersey over the weekend as Can’t Get You Out Of My Head singer joined Coldplay onstage.

1 Gwyneth Paltrow

This list would not be complete without the mother of Chris Martin’s children, his queen for a decade. Chris Martin and Goop got married in December 2003 after one year of getting together on October 2002. The couple however decided to call it quits after Paltrow filed for divorce in 2014 and was finalized in April 2015. The two had two beautiful children together named Apple Blythe Alison who is currently 18 years old and Moses Bruce Anthony who is now 16 years old.
