Jason Derulo is a prominent figure in the music industry, boasting a massive following on social media, with devoted fans who eagerly anticipate his projects. Derulo has long been in the spotlight, not just for his music, but also for the complexities of his romantic entanglements.

Derulo's career has often been overshadowed by conflicts, and this time, his complex romantic history unveils a narrative shrouded in controversy, marked by past relationships, cheating accusations, and alleged inappropriate behavior.

Who Is Jason Derulo's Girlfriend Now?

In June 2023, Jason Derulo published his book, titled Sing Your Name Out Loud: 15 Rules for Living Your Dream. This literary endeavor lays bare his life's rollercoaster ride, complete with highs, lows, and the keys to his success.

Derulo shared with People the insights into his current perspective on dating and reflected on a decade of transformation since his debut single, "Whatcha Say," soared to the top of the charts.

"Dating is definitely not a focus for me. In those places I just let life happen, and I'm not necessarily looking for anything at all. So it's my eye on the ball right now, honestly," Derulo emphasized.

The hitmaker reflected on his intense career focus, but he also expressed contentment and lack of regrets on his commitment to enduring relationships, especially with family, whom he considers his steadfast companions despite occasional missed opportunities due to his career-driven lifestyle.

Are Cheating Allegations The Reason Why Jason Derulo Broke Up With Ex-Girlfriend Jena Frumes?

In March 2020, Jason Derulo and Jena Frumes began their relationship, they spent the quarantine together and gained widespread attention for their TikTok videos during the pandemic. Their bond grew stronger and welcomed their first child, Jason King, on May 8, 2021.

However, they broke up in September 2021 with Derulo announcing their split on his Instagram. In 2022, Frumes slammed an Instagram follower who attempted to label her inappropriately.

The follower questioned her worth, citing her unmarried status and her son's situation. In response, Frumes clarified that she once aspired to marry Derulo but decided to end the relationship due to what she described as constant disrespect and infidelity.

Frumes asserted that she didn't want to be a single mom, and her primary desire was to create a loving family with Derulo. However, she felt his alleged infidelity and lack of respect hindered that dream. She stressed that women shouldn't be judged harshly, emphasizing the challenges they face in relationships.

Inside Jason Derulo's Lawsuit That Accused Him Of Exploiting An Aspiring Musician

In a lawsuit filed in October 2023, it was alleged that Jason Derulo traumatized aspiring popstar Emaza Gibson after she signed a deal with him in 2021, only to realize that he expected sexual favors in return.

According to NBC, the lawsuit was filed in Los Angeles County Court which claims that Derulo's request to initiate a joint venture with Gibson came in August 2021.

According to the lawsuit, Gibson agreed to produce a mixtape within four months and an album over half a year. It further alleges that Gibson was plied with excessive amounts of alcohol during a studio meeting in September 2021, after which Derulo allegedly made a suggestive remark that Gibson interpreted as a demand for sexual favors in exchange for success in the music industry.

In November 2021, Gibson decided to stand up against Derulo's advances, and she expressed her disappointment with the way she claimed she was treated by the singer. Gibson's emotional account of the situation, including the alleged toxic work environment, was shared in interviews and on her YouTube channel.

"It's unfortunate. I really looked up to him, I was a big fan of his music, you know, seeing a black man in the pop space, not doing the regular music like the rap…" Gibson told Sports and Hip-Hop with DJ Mad Max.

Jason Derulo denied the sexual harassment allegations lodged against him by Emaza Gibson.

Derulo shared a statement through his Instagram and expressed his deep offense at the claims, while his representative issued a statement refuting the accusations.

In return, Gibson's attorney called for accountability and described the need for a #MeToo movement in the music industry, condemning Derulo's alleged behavior, which includes broken promises, contract breaches, threats of physical harm, and inappropriate advances.

Jason Derulo Shut Down Rumors About His Controversial Split From Jordin Sparks

In 2014, Jason Derulo set the record straight when he spoke to ABC News and addressed the buzz surrounding his split from Jordin Sparks.

He explained, "Though I think it is a private matter and intended to keep my break-up with Jordin personal, due to bogus and irresponsible lies being reported by insensitive media outlets it became necessary for me to comment." Derulo wanted to dispel the speculation, which had alleged that infidelity and his burgeoning music career were to blame for the end of their three-year relationship.

In an interview on KIIS FM, Derulo elaborated on the complexities of their relationship. "There was a lot of tension in our relationship for a lot of different reasons. Every relationship has ups and downs when you stop having more good times than bad times, it’s time to call it quits."
