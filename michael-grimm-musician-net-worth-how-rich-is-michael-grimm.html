Michael Grimm (Musician) Net Worth: How rich is Michael Grimm?

 Posted on July 8, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  181 words  |  Martina Birk

Popular singer, Michael Grimm, who won season 5 of America’s Got Talent, reportedly has an estimated net worth of around $1 million. However, some sources claim that it might be less than that.

Grimm’s prize for winning the competition included $1 million (before taxes and reduction for lump sum payment) and the opportunity to headline the AGT Tour Show, including one show at Caesar’s Palace in Las Vegas on October 8, 2010.

On September 29, 2010, he signed a record deal with Epic Records. He and Smokey Robinson raised over $1 million during the 37th Annual Candlelight Concert on December 3, 2010.

Grimm released a self-titled album on May 17, 2011. The album debuted at No. 13 on US Pop Music Charts on May 26, 2011, selling over 23,000 copies. According to the Billboard Top 200, his album was No. 13 the week of June 4, 2011.

The singer is currently battling health-related issues and has called for financial assistance from his fans and loved ones. His wife, Lucie shared that people can donate via Venmo, PayPal, Attn, and also purchase his music.
