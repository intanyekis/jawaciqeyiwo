Of Mia Farrow's Many Children, Which Ones Is She Close With?

 Posted on August 28, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  823 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Mia Farrow has been acting in television, films, and theater since the 1960s to present day, and is best known as Rosemary in Rosemary's Baby. Mia has also been a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador since 2000, working to raise funds and awareness for children in conflict-affected regions to eradicate polio. She received accolades and awards for her work and was named one of the most influential people in the world by Time Magazine in 2008.

The list below includes details about each of Mia's fourteen children, the relationship shared between them, and fun facts on what they've been up to. From her first biological twins born in 1970, to her last child adopted in 1995, here are the children that Mia Farrow is closest with.

8 Matthew & Sascha Previn

Mia's oldest biological children are a set of twins named Matthew and Sascha Previn, born in 1970 to Mia and her second husband, Andre Previn. Matthew graduated from Georgetown University Law Center and is practicing law as a Partner at Paul Hastings, LLP. Sascha graduated from Fordham University, then began working as a teacher, and is currently a stay-at-home father. Both are close with their mother and stood by her in publicly denouncing Woody Allen.

7 Lark Song Previn

Lark Song Previn was the first of Mia’s children to be adopted into the family in 1973, and died at age 35 in 2008. Mia shared sentiments on Twitter, “My daughter Lark was an extraordinary woman, a wonderful daughter, sister, partner and mother… Despite her illness she lived a fruitful and loving life with her children and longtime partner. She succumbed to her illness & died suddenly in the hospital on Christmas, in her partner’s arms.”

6 Fletcher Previn

Fletcher Previn is Mia’s third biological child, who was born in 1974 and is also the shared son of her second husband, composer Andre Previn. Fletcher graduated from Connecticut College and is now serving as the Chief Information Officer and an executive sponsor for Cisco working with US Government agencies. Fletcher shares a close bond with his mother and supported her in denouncing Woody Allen in a signed statement with several of his other siblings.

5 Summer 'Daisy' Song & Soon-Yi Previn

Summer 'Daisy' Song and Soon-Yi Previn were the second and third children adopted by Mia and André in 1976 and 1978 from Vietnam and Korea. They garnered controversy when arrested for shoplifting $342 worth of lingerie from Christian Dior, and later for Soon-Yi's relationship with Mia's former partner, Woody Allen. Daisy is an actress best known for her role in Hannah and Her Sisters (1986) and Soon-Yi has been married to Woody Allen since 1992.

4 Moses & Dylan Farrow

Moses and Dylan Farrow were adopted in 1980 and 1985 from Korea and Texas by Mia and her long-term partner of the time, Woody Allen. Moses publicly spoke about his mother, accusing her of 'brainwashing' the children into believing Woody abused Dylan, who confirms being abused by her adoptive father. Moses is a therapist and adoption program coordinator for Lutheran Social Services of New England, and Dylan is a writer, mother, and sexual assault activist.

3 Satchel 'Ronan' O'Sullivan Farrow

Ronan Farrow was born in 1987 and is the only biological child of Mia and Woody Allen, however speculations exist about Frank Sinatra fathering him. Ronan is a journalist best known for the investigative reporting of sexual abuse allegations against Harvey Weinstein, which was published in The New Yorker. Ronan is close with Mia and has been estranged from Woody, saying, "He's my father married to my sister… That is such a moral transgression."

2 Tam Farrow

Tam Farrow was adopted in 1992 from Vietnam solely by Mia after news was released about Woody's affair with Soon-Yi . Tam was blind, suffered from multiple disabilities, and passed away at age seventeen in 2000. Mia shared, "My beloved daughter Tam passed away at seventeen from an accidental prescription overdose related to the agonizing migraines she suffered, and her heart ailment." However, Mia's son Moses later revealed that Tam's death was actually related to suicide.

1 Isaiah, Quincy, Thaddeus, & Frankie-Minh Farrow

Isaiah, Quincy, Thaddeus, and Frankie-Minh Farrow are the last of Mia's children to have been adopted solely by her in 1992, 1994, and 1995 respectively. Isaiah was named Time's 100 Most Influential People in 2012, Quincy attends college in Connecticut, Thaddeus committed suicide in 2016, and Frankie's life remains private. They are each very close with their mother and Isaiah and Quincy also stood by her in denouncing Woody Allen along with their other siblings.
