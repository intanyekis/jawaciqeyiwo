Ever since Gurfateh Pirzada rose to prominence, fans have been curious about his personal life. Let’s take a closer Look at his loving parents, faithful family and strong religious beliefs. 

Gurfateh Singh Pirzada is an Indian Actor and model known for his roles in Brahmāstra (2022), Guilty (2020), Hum Bhi Akele Tum Bhi Akele (2019), and Friends in Law (2018).

Born in 1996 in a Sikh family to an agriculturist and realtor Father and a housewife mother, Gurfateh made his acting debut in Friends in Law (2018). 

Then he later starred in several popular films, including the Netflix film “Guilty.”

The star of ‘Bombairiya,’ Gurfateh Pirzada, has been making waves in the Indian film industry for a few years now.

His captivating performances have made him a beloved Actor in the hearts of countless fans.

But behind the famous persona stands two individuals that raised him to be the amazing young man we see today – Gurfateh’s parents, Gurlal Pirzada and Paramjit Kaur Pirzada.

Meet Gurfateh Pirzada Parents & Family: Gurlal Pirzada & Paramjit Kaur Pirzada

 Gurlal Pirzada is an incredible Father who always supported his son’s aspirations and encouraged him to pursue these dreams.

Having seen Gurfateh’s immense potential and raw talent, Gurlal decided to move to Mumbai and help his son fulfill his goals.

He was always there to lend a helping hand whenever Gurfateh needed assistance.

Gurlal Pirzada may be Gurfateh’s adoring Father, but Paramjit Kaur Pirzada is his devoted mother.

From the start, Paramjit has always been her son’s most significant source of strength and inspiration.

Even when times were tough, she never wavered in her support and offered him.

Gurlal and Paramjit form the nucleus of Gurfateh’s tight-knit family. His siblings include sister Simreet Kaur and brother Harwinder Singh.

The Pirzada’s have been together through thick and thin, standing firmly by each other’s side.

Family is more than just a concept to them; it is their lifeblood, and this is most evident in how they interact.

What is Gurfateh Pirzada Riligion?

The Pirzada family follows the Sikh religion and instills religious solid values in their children.
