Barton, 24, showed off her curves in super-tight, pale yellow denim, bright red shoes and a midriff-baring top while on a Starbucks run Sunday afternoon.

Pointed out Allieiswired.com, "Mischa Barton was out and about, dressed like this. Showing off her flabby belly… Mischa should probably rethink that Starbucks, eh?

GossipCenter.com called the "belly-baring top…unflattering," and PrimaryBeats captioned a photo, "OMG What happened to her? Check out poor Mischa Barton being a mess and showing her extra body weight while going out to get some coffee at Starbucks. [Her short-lived CW drama] The Beautiful Life should've never got canceled!"

Last Thursday, Radaronline.com highlighted her "softer, more voluptuous look" as she ran errands in Beverly Hills.
